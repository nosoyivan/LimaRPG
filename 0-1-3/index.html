    How to Play:

    Combat Overview:

    During combat, you have three main options to choose from:

    • - Bite: A basic attack that deals damage ranging from 3 to 6 (1D6).
    • - Scratch: A quick double attack that deals two hits, each dealing 1 to 4 damage (2D4).
    • - Kick Dirt: Kick dirt into you opponent and cause their accuracy to drastically fall.
    • - Potion: Use a potion to restore 10 HP (+10HP). You start each level with a limited number of potions.

    The combat log will show the latest moves at the top, helping you track the flow of battle.

    Battle Example:

    1. Enemy's Attack dealt 3 DMG to You : 17HP
    2. You regained 10HP from a potion. 1 Potion left
    3. Enemy's attack missed!
    4. Your Scratch dealt 4 + 3 DMG to Enemy : 8HP
    5. Enemy's Attack dealt 4 DMG to You : 16HP
    6. Your Bite dealt 5 DMG to Enemy : 15HP
    7. You've encountered a creature! The battle begins:
    This is the Creature Badge:
    Enemy 20HP Level 1

    It displays the current creature's base stats, including health and level.
    Leveling:

    Leveling up resets your potions and grants +2HP +1 DMG per level.

    Events:

    While exploring, you may find sidequests and rescue missions. Some offer rewards, others present challenges.
